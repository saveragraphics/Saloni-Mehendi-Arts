Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Does the artist travel to my location?+
Yes! We provide full home service across Bhopal and surrounding areas. Additional travel charges may apply outside Bhopal city limits. Please contact us to confirm your area.
How long does bridal mehendi last?+
Our premium mehendi typically lasts 2–3 weeks with proper care. The color deepens for the first 24–48 hours. Avoid water contact for at least 8 hours after application for best results.
What is the booking process?+
Fill out our booking form or WhatsApp us with your event date, location, and design preference. We'll confirm availability and send a quote within 24 hours. A small advance secures your booking.
Is the mehendi safe for sensitive skin?+
We use 100% natural, chemical-free mehendi cones. If you have known skin allergies, we recommend a patch test 24 hours before. Black/chemical mehendi is never used.
How far in advance should I book?+
For weddings and peak season (October–March), book at least 4–8 weeks in advance. For other occasions, 1–2 weeks notice is usually sufficient, subject to availability.
Do you take group / Mehendi party bookings?+
Absolutely! We accommodate group bookings, Sangeet functions, and corporate events. Multiple artists can be arranged for large parties. Contact us for group pricing.
